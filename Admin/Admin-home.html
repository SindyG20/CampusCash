Steps to Maintain Employees

1
Access Employee Records

Navigate to the Employees section from the main dashboard to view all employee details.

2
Search or Filter

Use the search bar or filters to find specific employees by name, ID, role, or department.

3
Edit Employee Details

Click on an employee to update their personal information, role, permissions, or employment status.

4
Assign Roles and Permissions

Ensure each employee has the correct system access based on their responsibilities.

5
Review and Save Changes

Always review all changes before saving. Document updates if necessary for compliance.

Important Notes

  • Verify employee ID before making any updates
  • Maintain confidentiality of employee information
  • Document any changes for audit purposes
  • Contact IT support if any issues arise while maintaining records

Steps to Manage Appointments

1
Access Appointment Records

Navigate to the Appointments section from the main dashboard to view all scheduled appointments.

2
Schedule New Appointments

Click "Add Appointment" and fill in the details such as student/staff name, date, time, and purpose.

3
Edit Existing Appointments

Select an appointment to change its details, reschedule, or cancel if necessary.

4
Confirm Appointment Status

Ensure appointments are confirmed and updated in the system to avoid overlaps or conflicts.

5
Review and Save Changes

Double-check all updates before saving. Keep a record of any changes for reference.

Important Notes

  • Verify all appointment dates and times before confirming
  • Ensure no double bookings occur for staff or students
  • Maintain confidentiality of appointment details
  • Document any changes or cancellations for records
